## 🎨 1. Visual Identity & Design System
The new design follows the **"Edge Analytics"** aesthetic—a dark, high-contrast theme optimized for hardware and AI engineering contexts.

*   **Primary Palette:** 
    *   Background: `#020617` (Deep Midnight)
    *   Primary Accent: `#3b82f6` (Neon Blue)
    *   Secondary Accent: `#22c55e` (Emerald Green for IoT status)
*   **Typography:** 
    *   Headings: Montserrat / Inter (Font-weight: 800)
    *   Content: JetBrains Mono / Inter (Clean and technical)
    *   *System:* Forced tracking-tighter and italicized secondary headings for an aggressive, modern look.
*   **Design Tokens:** 
    *   Glassmorphism: `backdrop-filter: blur(12px)` + `bg-white/5`
    *   Elevation: Custom shadows with neon primary/secondary glows for interactive depth.
